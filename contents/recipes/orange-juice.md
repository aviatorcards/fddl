# Orange Juice

A refreshing glass of orange juice.

## Ingredients

- 2 oranges
- 1 cup of water
- 1 tablespoon of honey

## Instructions

1. Wash the oranges.
2. Cut the oranges into small pieces.
3. Add the oranges to a blender.
4. Add the water and honey.
5. Blend until smooth.
6. Pour into a glass.
