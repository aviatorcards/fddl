# Midnight Chocolate Cake

This cake is dense, fudge-like, and surprisingly easy to make. Perfect when you've finally squashed that bug and need a celebration.

## Ingredients

- 8 oz Bittersweet chocolate (70% cacao)
- 1 cup Unsalted butter
- 1 1/2 cups Granulated sugar
- 3 Large eggs
- 1/2 cup Dutch-processed cocoa powder
- 1 tsp Sea salt

## Instructions

1.  **Prep**: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease an 8-inch round pan.
2.  **Melt**: Combine chocolate and butter in a heat-safe bowl over simmering water. Stir until smooth.
3.  **Mix**: Remove from heat and whisk in sugar. Add eggs one at a time.
4.  **Fold**: Sift in cocoa powder and salt. Gently fold until just combined.
5.  **Bake**: Pour into pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.

### Serving Suggestion
Serve with a dollop of cold mascarpone and a double espresso.
