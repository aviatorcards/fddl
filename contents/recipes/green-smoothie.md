# Brain Booster Smoothie

When you're deep in the "zone," you need fuel that sustains energy without the brain fog.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups Baby spinach
- 1 Green apple (cored)
- 1/2 Avocado (for healthy fats)
- 1 scoop Vegan protein powder
- 1 tbsp Chia seeds
- 12 oz Coconut water

## Instructions

1.  **Liquid First**: Add coconut water to the blender.
2.  **Greens**: Add spinach and blend on high until smooth (no chunks!).
3.  **Solids**: Add apple, avocado, and chia seeds.
4.  **Finish**: Add protein powder and blend on low for 10 seconds.
